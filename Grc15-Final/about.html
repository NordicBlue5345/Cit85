My Name Is Cassandra

I was born in Tulare Ca in 1993. I grew up in the most local cities, the main being

  • Clovis
  • Fresno
  • Tulare

As a child I went to Walters, McCardle, Sierra Vista, and Linclon Elementary for grades K-6. After that my family moved and I started at Rio Vista for 7th and 8th. From there I met my forever best friend Stacey, and Nick my Future Husband. I have so many wonderful memories growing up and just going wherever me and Stacey thought would be fun that day. Her mom, in a way adopted me and to this day is such a wonderful and amazing woman, and I still call her Mama.

I moved 2 weeks into my sophmore year and ended up at Clovis North High School. Here I would finish out the remainder of my school years until I graduated in June of 2012. To be honest a large part of my journey was kinda boring after that. I moved to Visalia/Tulare and ended up working at a resturant called Bravo Farms for a little while.
