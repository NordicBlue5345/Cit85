My Hobbies

There're a lot of things I love to spend my time doing!

Taking Photos

I love using my camera and taking pictures of the world around me. My main source of insparation is my children and nature. My biggest thing is capturing the little moments that are easily missed.

Gardening

I love to Garden and grow food or flowers. It is a new hobby for me and I am learning new things everyday, but the best part is seeing my plants get bigger and bigger. Things I love to grow the most is Veggies, Flowers and Herbs.

Crafting

I love all things art and things that have to do with color! My favorite color is blue but I love the color schemes of Fall and Winter. I also enjoy creating things for special occations and parties. I've made things from blankets, to centerpieces for my sons birthdays, to a Loki headpiece for Halloween.
