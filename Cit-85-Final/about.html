All About Me

Hello, my name is Cassandra. I am 29 years old and born in the farming town of Tulare. I am married to my husband Nick and Mother to two wonderful little boys. I will talk more about them on another page.

I have lived in the local cities of Fresno, Clovis, Tulare and Visalia but it is Clovis North High School, Go Broncos!, that I graduated from in 2012. From there I have done my best to find my way in this world, though some moments were harder than others.

Meet My Besties

Stacey is my person, I have known her since we were 12 years old and she knows me better then anyone else out there. I can talk to her on just about anything and she never judges. We can have different views but the love is still there. She is more my sister then a friend and even her Mom considers me her daughter.

My 2nd main Bestie is Sevannah. While we havent known each other for very long, a thing I love about her is she is honest. While I dont always like to hear it, she knows when to tell me im being ridiculous. I would not be here in College today if she had not pushed me to at least apply and From there the rest is history.
